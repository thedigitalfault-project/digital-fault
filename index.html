INTRODUCTION

Unless otherwise specified, questions referring to screen use or digital use primarily relate to personal phone/social media/entertainment use rather than mandatory academic or school-related use.
  • This survey aims to understand the impact of digital habits on teenagers.
  • Please answer all questions as honestly and accurately as possible. Your responses are completely anonymous and cannot be linked to your identity.
  • If you have already completed this survey, please do not submit another response, as this helps maintain the accuracy of the data.
  • The results will be used for a student-led initiative focused on research and social awareness.
  • Please respond sincerely. Deliberately inaccurate or misleading answers may reduce the reliability of the findings.
  • This survey was created as part of The Digital Fault Project, a student-led initiative exploring digital habits among teenagers.
